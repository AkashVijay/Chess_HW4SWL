All about Hikaru

About him

Hikaru Nakamura is an American chess grandmaster, streamer, and five-time U.S. Chess Champion. He became a grandmaster at 15 and holds a peak rating of 2816. Nakamura is also the reigning World Fischer Random Chess Champion and has represented the U.S. in seven Chess Olympiads, winning one team gold and two team bronze medals.

Internet

Hikaru Nakamura, sponsored by Chess.com, began streaming on Twitch as "GMHikaru" in 2018. He plays speed chess, often against grandmasters, streamers, and subscribers, sometimes using handicaps like blindfolded play or piece odds. His Twitch channel gained significant popularity during the COVID-19 pandemic, with a 10x increase in audience size from February to June 2020. By September he had 500,000 followers. Nakamura prioritizes his streaming career over professional chess and joined the esports team TSM in 2020, followed by Misfits Gaming in 2022.
